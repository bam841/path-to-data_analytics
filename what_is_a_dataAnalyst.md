# Data analytics
a data analytics job or role is to collect data for a company. they are the one that turns raw data into actionable and usable insights/information. they gather data from various sources and they use tools for 
organizing the data that they handle. they do tasks like cleaning data sets remove errors, outliers and duplicates. their work usually involves using  excel,sql,and python.
