# this is for breaking down the types of analytics
### there are four categories of analytics
#### 1. descriptive
- descriptive analysis is more on what happen?
 for further understanding, imagine this scenario. you are currently under a checkup and the nurse tells youy that your blood pressure is not normal and
  descriptive
 " your blood pressure is currently 140/90"
  descriptive is like where the narrative of the data starts\

#### 2. Diagnostic 
 -- diagnosic is more on why did this happen?
 - refering from the scenario diagnostic will be:

        you ate lots of food rich in sodium (salt), added sugars, and unhealthy saturated or trans fats
        now this would be the Diagnostic in this case. it is more likely looking out for the cause of the problem

#### 3. predicitve
-- now predictive would then be, what will happen next?
  if we are referring from the scenario earlier the predictive analysis would look like this:

    if you keep on this unhealthy diet you are most likely to have serious disease and will get hosptalized 

#### 4. prescriptive
--  prescriptive is more on about what will be the course of action / what to do?
  - from the word itself prescriptive it is finding ways to preven what would happen in the predicitve analysis

        in this case the doctor would then give you medicine that can help you lower your blood pressure
        and you will also be given a strict diet that you need to follow to avoid having high blood pressure
